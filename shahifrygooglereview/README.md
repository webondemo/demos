# Shahi Fry Center Review App

A simple app for Shahi Fry Center Restaurant to collect and manage customer reviews.

## Features

- View customer reviews
- Add new reviews
- Rate food and service
- Easy and user-friendly interface

## Purpose

This app helps Shahi Fry Center understand customer feedback, improve service quality, and provide a better dining experience.
